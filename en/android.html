Click here or scan the QRCode below to download Android APP on Google Play



Once downloaded, launch Android APP, and click the menu on the top right to open the "account management". Make sure your phone is in the same network with your computer, it will find the "Lomorage Photo Assistant" installed on computer, select "Lomorage" in the top list, and then create an account.




After logging in, it will take a few minutes to prepare for photo backup, once finished, you can drag down to start the backup process.




If you need to create another user, click the menu on the top right to open the "account management", and create a new user there.
