Click here or scan the QRCode below to download Lomorage iOS APP from Apple APP Store.



Once installed, launch the iOS APP, if your phone is in the same network with the computer which has the "Lomorage Photo Assistant" running, it should be able to find it, and select "Lomorage“ in the top list，click "Next" and then create an account.






After logging in, it will take a few minutes to prepare for photo backup, once finished, you can drag down to start the backup process.




If you need to create another user, you can go to the setting tab in Lomorage APP, and click the "Create User" button to create a new user.
