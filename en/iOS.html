Key Features:

  • Optimized for Massive Syncs: Engineered to handle large photo libraries (18,000+ photos) seamlessly.
  • Apple Live Photo Support: Natively bundles Live Photos (paired static HEIC/JPG and high-frame-rate MOV video) into ZIP archives to ensure full backup integrity.
  • High-Performance Native Hashing: Leverages a custom native module to perform fast and secure file hashing.
  • Auto-Discovery (mDNS): Automatically detects your local Lomorage Photo Assistant server when connected to the same Wi-Fi.
  • Multi-User Support: Easily manage and create multiple user accounts to share the storage while keeping files private.

How to Connect:

  1. Open the app on your iPhone.
  2. Ensure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi network as the computer running the Lomorage Photo Assistant.
  3. The app will automatically discover your server. Select it from the list and follow the prompts to create or log in to your account.
